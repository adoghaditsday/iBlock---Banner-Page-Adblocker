# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 20, 2026**

iBlock – Banner and Page Ad Blocker processes supported advertising elements locally in the browser.

## Data stored locally

The extension may store:

- whether blocking is enabled;
- Regular or Super mode selection;
- video-player protection preference;
- up to 50 whitelisted domains;
- a local, deduplicated list of sites where off-player ad-related activity was detected.

This information is stored with `chrome.storage.local`.

## Data not collected

The extension does not include analytics, advertising, remote telemetry, an account system, or a server-side database. It does not sell personal information or transmit browsing history to the developer.

## Permissions

The extension requires access to visited pages and request filtering so it can perform its advertised banner and page-ad blocking function. See [PERMISSIONS.md](PERMISSIONS.md).
