# Suggested Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name

iBlock – Banner and Page Ad Blocker

## Summary

Blocks supported third-party ad requests and reduces high-confidence banners, popups, native ads, and rich-media promotions outside protected video players.

## Description

iBlock – Banner and Page Ad Blocker reduces supported advertising while preserving normal website functionality. It blocks selected third-party advertising and tracking requests and removes high-confidence banner, popup, native, and rich-media advertising outside protected video players.

Regular mode provides conservative everyday filtering. Super mode enables broader request filtering and stronger off-player cleanup for ad-heavy websites. Detected video players, navigation, forms, menus, content listings, product grids, galleries, and same-site cards are protected to reduce compatibility problems.

Users can whitelist up to 50 websites. Settings are stored locally in Chrome. No account is required, and the extension does not include remote analytics or telemetry.

Advertising systems differ between websites, so results may vary. iBlock does not claim to remove every advertisement, bypass anti-adblock systems, or modify advertisements inserted directly into protected video streams.
